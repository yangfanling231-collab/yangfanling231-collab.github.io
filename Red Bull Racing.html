Red Bull Racing

Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen, born in 1997 in Belgium and racing under the Dutch flag, is the lead driver for Red Bull Racing. He made his Formula 1 debut in 2015 at just 17 years old, becoming the youngest driver in the sport’s history. Verstappen joined Red Bull in 2016 and won his first race with the team in Spain, immediately proving his talent. Known for his aggressive driving style, consistency, and determination, he became World Champion in 2021 after a dramatic season finale. Since then, Verstappen has established himself as the dominant force in modern F1, securing multiple consecutive titles and breaking numerous records for wins, poles, and fastest laps.

Yuki Tsunoda

Yuki Tsunoda, born in 2000 in Kanagawa, Japan, made his Formula 1 debut in 2021 and quickly impressed with his aggressive yet precise racing style. After several seasons with Red Bull’s sister team, he earned promotion to Red Bull Racing in 2025. Tsunoda represents a new generation of talent, combining speed, adaptability, and determination. As the first Japanese driver to race for Red Bull, he brings a fresh dynamic to the team alongside Verstappen, and has become a symbol of inspiration for fans across Asia and the world.

Red Bull F1 car

Red Bull’s Formula 1 car is renowned for its aggressive aerodynamic design and innovative engineering. The car features a highly optimized chassis that maximizes downforce and cornering speed while maintaining strong straight-line performance. Known for flexibility and creativity in design, Red Bull Racing consistently showcases outstanding competitiveness on the track thanks to its talented engineering team and advanced technology.
