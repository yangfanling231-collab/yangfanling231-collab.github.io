Scuderia Ferrari

Charles Leclerc

Charles Leclerc, whose full name is Charles Marc Herve Perceval Leclerc, was born on October 16, 1997, in Monte Carlo, Monaco. He is a current Formula One (F1) driver for Scuderia Ferrari. He began karting at a young age, winning multiple championships as a teenager before gradually moving up the ranks, excelling in GP3 and F2. In 2018, Leclerc entered F1 with Sauber and was quickly signed by Ferrari due to his impressive performances, becoming one of the most anticipated young drivers in recent years. Known for his speed and strong qualifying performances, he has won numerous races throughout his career and finished second in the 2022 F1 World Championship. A Monegasque star, he is not only a key player for Ferrari but also one of the most promising championship contenders today.

Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton, full name Lewis Carl Davidson Hamilton, was born on January 7, 1985, in Stevenage, England. He is a current Formula One (F1) driver for Mercedes. He entered F1 as a rookie in 2007 and won his first world championship the following year (2008), becoming the youngest driver to win the title at the time. Hamilton is known for his consistent performance, exceptional technique in wet races, and his ability to overtake. From 2014 to 2020, he and Mercedes dominated the F1 field, winning seven world championships (tied with Michael Schumacher for the most in history). He also holds numerous records throughout his career, including most race wins and most pole positions. Hamilton is not only a top driver on the track but also a public figure promoting diversity and equality, and is considered one of the greatest drivers in F1 history.

Ferrari F1 Car

Ferrari's Formula 1 car is renowned for its iconic Rosso Corsa livery and cutting-edge engineering. The latest model emphasizes aerodynamic efficiency, downforce, and straight-line speed, continuing the team's long tradition of technical innovation.
